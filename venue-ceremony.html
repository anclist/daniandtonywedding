Venue Details

El Gasco is a palatial mansion with over 100 years, it's located in Torrelodones, at km 27 of the A6 highway, a 20 minutes drive from Madrid.

The Estate sits at the highest point of a protected park, so the views are impressive.
‍
It was the residence of the Marquis of Valterra, with its granite blocks and noble woods it still retains the charm and elegance of yesteryear.

A magical place nestled in a natural park where nature is the protagonist.

Please join us to celebrate this special day of our lives
